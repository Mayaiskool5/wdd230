Temple Inn & Suites

~Full-Time Temple Missionary~

Junior Suite

Sleeps: 6
-This room features an open floor plan with easy access from living area and the bedrooms. This will have a smaller living area than the Suite options below, in this room they are an extention of the bedroom space and does not include a dining area. Comes with an optional sofa bed.

Residential Suite

Sleeps: Up to 8
-These fully furnished suites come with a seperate living room from the bedroom spaces. They also include a dining area for eating and comfort and one or more bathrooms. Some may include fully stocked kitchens, in-suite dryer units, and even patios and terraces.

Presidential Suite

Sleeps: 2-9
-This is the absolute best suite in the hotel!
-This hotel only has a few Presidential Suites. This suite comes with anything you can think of, from expansing living areas, seperate dining area, fully stocked fridge and kitchen, blow dryer, high-end toiletries, jetted tubs in bathrooms that look like spas, in-suite washer and dryer units, entry foyers, private patios and/or terraces, fabulous panoramic views and many other amenities.
-Some rooms come with private Butlers!

Connecting Suite

Has 2+ rooms connected!
-These rooms will be connected in the living area of the suites.
-Connecting suites will almost always come cheaper than a regular two-bedroom suite.
-These rooms will come stocked as standard rooms, the only different is the connected rooms so that your parties can navigate with each other more easily.
