Oakland, CA Temple Info

Dedicated: 17-19 November 1964 by David O. McKay
Rededicated: May 2019
Total Floor Area: 95,000 square feet
Fun Fact: This is a very unique feature of the Oakland Temple because it is the only temple in the world with a rooftop garden, and it's open to the public.
Other Info: Commanding a sweeping view of the San Francisco Bay Area, the 170-foot Oakland California Temple is a highly recognized East Bay landmark. Also contains 4 ordinance rooms and seven sealing rooms.
